When life gives you lemons make cake

Lemontime is a hilarious podcast where two lifelong friends dive into the world of music with quirky banter and laugh-out-loud moments. They argue over boy band hair, share embarrassing concert stories, and invite guests to talk about the songs that mean the most to them. Each episode is a blend of humor and heartfelt moments, making it a must-listen for music lovers. Tune in for a musical adventure that’s as entertaining as it is enlightening.
